DO YOU LOVE LIVE MUSIC?

We do! We think live music is good for just about everything: stress, your sense of humor, your peace of mind and most especially for your 'head'. We still remember Grace Slick singing that song & friends...nothing has changed. The revolution is not over. Goodness. Life is still cool and old school bars are fun. Let's just say that we dig musicians of all ages coming to the Outta the Way Cafe and grooving on their music. Live Rock & Roll, Blues, Country, Bluegrass and Folk musicians all find their way to The Outta The Way Cafe to play their music for you.

Welcome Friend! Let's All Play!!
